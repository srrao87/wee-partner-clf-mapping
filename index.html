Women's Economic Empowerment Partners' Forum

The Women's Economic Empowerment Partners' Forum brings together Gates Foundation-supported organizations working to strengthen women's livelihoods, agency, and economic participation. Partner organizations engage with Cluster Level Federations (CLFs), district systems, and community institutions to support implementation, learning, and scale. This dashboard maps the geographic reach of partner engagement, the distribution of CLFs in the available datasets, and areas where multiple partners are working with the same CLFs.
